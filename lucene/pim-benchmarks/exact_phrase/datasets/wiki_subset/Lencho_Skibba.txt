'''Lencho Skibba''' ({{lang-am|ለንችሆ ጽኪብባ}}, born 18 February 1988 in [[Dire Dawa]]) is an [[Ethiopia]]n [[association football|football]] midfielder.

Skibba began his career with [[USL First Division]] side [[Minnesota Thunder]], and joined [[Kastoria F.C.]] in the Greek [[Beta Ethniki]] for the 2007–08 season.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=EPAE|script-title=el:ΠΑΝΣΕΡΡΑΙΚΟΣ ΜΓΣ ΠΑΕ - ΚΑΣΤΟΡΙΑ ΠΑΕ|url=http://www.epae.org/match.fds?categ=2&dior_code=94&cal_day=5&game=6&group=0&type=1&pagecode=01.02&langid=1|language=Greek|accessdate=2009-10-12}}</ref>

Skibba played youth football in Ethiopia until he was adopted by [[Germany|Germa]]n parents in 2003. He played in the youth system for [[Alemannia Aachen]] until 2007, when he moved to the United States and joined the Minnesota Thunder.
