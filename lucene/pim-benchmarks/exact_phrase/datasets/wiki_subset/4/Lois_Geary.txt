'''''Lois Geary''''' (July 25, 1929 â€“ June 28, 2014) was an American actress of the stage and screen.

Geary was born in [[Fort Wayne, Indiana]] and moved to [[Santa Fe, New Mexico]] in the 1960s. She often worked in the area's scene, but would land small roles in films like ''[[The Astronaut Farmer]]'', ''[[Silverado (film)|Silverado]]'', ''[[Sunshine Cleaning]]'' and ''[[The Last Stand (2013 film)|The Last Stand]]''.

Geary died on June 28, 2014 at the age of 84.<ref>http://www.santafenewmexican.com/news/local_news/lois-geary---actress-activist-was-a-mainstay-in/article_1d52da54-1d62-5b25-8ac0-c3ea8103b85d.html</ref>
