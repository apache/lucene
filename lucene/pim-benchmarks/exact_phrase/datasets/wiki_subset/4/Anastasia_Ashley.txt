'''Anastasia Electra Ashley''' (born February 10, 1987) is an [[United States|American]] professional [[Surfing|surfer]] and model.<ref name="luckymag">{{cite web|url=http://www.luckymag.com/style/2014/08/anastasia-ashley-jewelry-line-interview|title=Pro Surfer Anastasia Ashley Interview|author=Hillary Daniels|publisher=luckymag.com|date=2014-09-19|accessdate=2014-09-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150112024138/http://www.luckymag.com/style/2014/08/anastasia-ashley-jewelry-line-interview|archive-date=2015-01-12|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="natgeo">{{cite web|url=http://adventureblog.nationalgeographic.com/2011/02/08/surfer-anastasia-ashley-on-big-wave-surfing-hawaiis-waimea-bay/|title=Surfer Anastasia Ashley on Big-Wave Surfing Hawaii’s Waimea Bay|author=Mary Anne Potts|publisher=adventureblog.nationalgeographic.com|date=2011-02-08|accessdate=2014-09-19}}</ref><ref name="grindtv">{{cite web|url=http://www.grindtv.com/action-sports/surf/post/the-inside-scoop-on-the-anastasia-ashley-wipeout/|title=The inside scoop on the Anastasia Ashley wipeout|author=Chris Cote|publisher=grindtv.com|date=2014-09-15|accessdate=2014-09-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150306171110/http://www.grindtv.com/action-sports/surf/post/the-inside-scoop-on-the-anastasia-ashley-wipeout/|archive-date=2015-03-06|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="si_official2">{{cite web|url=https://www.si.com/swim-daily/2015/04/07/anastasia-ashley-si-now-monster-waves-si-swimsuit-and-more|title=Anastasia Ashley talks body paint, monster waves & more on SI NOW|author=Kelsey Hendrix|publisher=si.com|date=2015-04-07|accessdate=2015-04-15}}</ref><ref name="the_inert">{{cite web|url=http://www.theinertia.com/surf/anastasia-ashley-talks-big-waves-and-body-paint-on-si-now/|title=Anastasia Ashley Talks Big Waves and Body Paint|publisher=theinertia.com|accessdate=2015-04-15}}</ref>

==Early life==
Ashley was born in [[San Clemente, California]] on February 10, 1987.<ref name="jettygirl">{{cite web|url=http://www.jettygirl.com/surfers/anastasia.ashley.page.3.html|title=This Month's JettyGirl: Anastasia Ashley|publisher=jettygirl.com|accessdate=2014-09-19}}</ref><ref name="askmen1">{{cite web|url=http://www.askmen.com/celebs/interview_200/219_anastasia_ashley_interview.html|title=13 Questions With Anastasia Ashley|publisher=askmen.com|accessdate=2014-09-19}}</ref><ref name="apoloformen">{{cite web|url= http://www.apoloformen.com/6-things-about-anastasia-ashley/|title=6 things you should know about Anastasia Ashley |publisher=apoloformen.com|accessdate=8 March 2016}}</ref> She began surfing at the age of 5 right before moving to Hawaii.<ref name="surfbang">{{cite web|url=http://surfbang.com/features/2010/03/interview-with-anastasia-ashley.html|title=Interview with Anastasia Ashley|author=John Zanussi|publisher=surfbang.com|date=2010-03-08|accessdate=2014-09-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150316181104/http://surfbang.com/features/2010/03/interview-with-anastasia-ashley.html|archive-date=2015-03-16|url-status=dead}}</ref> At the age of 6, she won a surf contest using a run-down board that she found in a trash can outside of her house.<ref name="espn1">{{cite web|url=http://espn.go.com/espnw/journeys-victories/article/7850043/anastasia-ashley-turns-surfing-get-tough-times|title=Surfing gets Anastasia Ashley through tough times|author=Cristina Goyanes|publisher=espn.go.com|date=2012-05-03|accessdate=2014-09-19}}</ref> By the age of 7, she picked up a sponsor to replace her run-down board with new ones.<ref name="espn1" />

==Career==
[[File:Web Summit 2017 - Surf Summit SM1 2988 (38134975446).jpg|thumb|left|upright=1.4|Ashley at Surf Summit, prior to [[Web Summit]] 2017, in [[Ericeira]], [[Portugal]]|alt=]]
In 2003, she received the Triple Crown Rookie of the Year award.<ref name="espn1"/> At the age of 16, Ashley won her first major national title.<ref name="independentIE" /> Later, she also won titles including two National Scholastic Surfing Association championships and the Professional Surfing Tour of America championship.<ref name="espn1"/> Ashley won the 2010 Pipeline Women's Pro in Hawaii.<ref name="luckymag" /> She appeared in the [[Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Edition]] in 2014.<ref name="huffpost1">{{cite web|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/02/20/anastasia-ashley-sports-illustrated-swimsuit-issue_n_4826406.html|title=Anastasia Ashley Lands Coveted Spot In 2014 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue|author=Chloe Fox|publisher=huffingtonpost.com|date=2014-02-20|accessdate=2014-09-19}}</ref> As of September 8, 2014, Ashley is ranked 59th in the 2014 Women's Qualification Series.<ref name="aspworldtour1">{{cite web|url=http://www.aspworldtour.com/athletes/tour/wqs?offset=51&sort=rank&year=2014|title=2014 Women's Qualification Series Rankings|publisher=aspworldtour.com|date=2014-09-08|accessdate=2014-09-25}}</ref>

In September 2014, she started her own ''Gypsy Jet Set'' jewelry line in collaboration with OK1984, a jewelry company.<ref name="luckymag" /><ref name="si2">{{cite web|url=https://www.si.com/swim-daily/2014/08/11/kate-upton-interrupted-abc-anastasia-ashley-jewelry-line|title=Kate Upton gets interrupted on ABC, Anastasia Ashley's new jewelry line and more Monday Swim news|publisher=si.com|date=2014-08-11|accessdate=2014-09-25}}</ref><ref name="bustle1">{{cite web|url=http://www.bustle.com/articles/34867-professional-surfer-anastasia-ashley-debuts-gypsy-jet-set-jewelry-line-and-were-stoked-about-the-results|title=Professional Surfer Anastasia Ashley Debuts Gypset Jet Set Jewelry Line and We're Stoked About the Results|author=Samantha Matthews|publisher=bustle.com|date=2014-07-01|accessdate=2014-09-25}}</ref> She was featured in the October 2014 issue of ''[[Maxim (magazine)|Maxim]]'' magazine.<ref name="prnews1">{{cite web|url=http://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/bikini-ready-lifestyle-brands-anastasia-ashley-featured-in-maxim-magazines-october-issue----on-newsstands-now-275427781.html|title=Bikini Ready® Lifestyle Brand's Anastasia Ashley Featured in Maxim Magazine's October Issue -- on Newsstands Now|publisher=prnewswire.com|date=2014-09-17|accessdate=2014-09-25}}</ref> In November 2014, she went to Ireland for a Web Summit on how to maintain a competitive edge in surfing with a focus on social media tools. Ashley was interested in convincing [[Taoiseach]] [[Enda Kenny]] to surf.<ref name="independentIE">{{cite web|url=http://www.independent.ie/style/fashion/style-talk/pro-surfer-anastasia-ashley-wants-to-hit-the-waves-with-taoiseach-30718716.html|title=Pro surfer Anastasia Ashley wants to hit the waves with Taoiseach|author=Caitlin McBride|publisher=independent.ie|date=2014-11-04|accessdate=2014-11-25}}</ref>

Ashley appeared on ''[[Hell's Kitchen (U.S. TV series)|Hell's Kitchen]]'' in the third episode of Season 14 where she taught the team challenge winners how to surf. She also attended as a dining room guest in that episode's dinner service.<ref name="fox1">{{cite web|url=http://www2.fox.com/watch/411826755657|title=A Star-Studded Night from "16 Chefs Compete"|publisher=fox.com|accessdate=2015-04-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150416010659/http://www2.fox.com/watch/411826755657|archive-date=2015-04-16|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref name="reviewf">{{cite web|url=http://reviewfix.com/2015/03/hells-kitchen-coverage-clean-some-fish/|title=Hell’s Kitchen Coverage: Clean Some Fish|publisher=reviewfix.com|date=2015-03-31|accessdate=2015-04-15}}</ref>

Ashley was a [[Vegetarianism|vegetarian]] and has posed for a [[People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals|PETA]]'s vegetarian ad campaign in 2006. She is now a vegan. <ref>{{cite web|url=http://en.protothema.gr/anastasia-ashley-a-hot-vegetarian/|title=Anastasia Ashley: A hot… vegetarian|publisher=protothema.gr|date=2014-12-09|accessdate=2018-03-12}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.peta2.com/news/anastasia-ashley-too-beautiful/|title=Anastasia Ashley: Too Beautiful|publisher=peta2.com|date=2012-01-19|accessdate=2018-03-12}}</ref>
