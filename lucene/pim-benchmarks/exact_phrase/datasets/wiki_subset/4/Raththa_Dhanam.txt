'''''Raththa Dhanam''''' (Tamil: ரத்ததானம்; English: ''Blood Donation'') is a [[Tamil films of 1988|1988]] [[Cinema of India|Indian]] [[Tamil language]] film, directed by [[Sivachandran]] and produced by himself and [[Lakshmi (actress)|Lakshmi]]. The film stars [[Prabhu (actor)|Prabhu]], [[Gautami]], [[Sadhana (Tamil actress)|Sadhana]], [[Lakshmi (actress)|Lakshmi]] and [[Sivachandran]] in lead roles. The film had musical score by [[Gangai Amaran]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://spicyonion.com/movie/ratha-dhanam/|title=Raththa Dhanam|accessdate=2014-09-19|publisher=spicyonion.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gomolo.com/raththa-dhanam-movie/11260|title=Raththa Dhanam|accessdate=2014-09-19|publisher=gomolo.com}}</ref> The film was dubbed in Telugu as ''Raktha Dhanam''.<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SUPFO0lcLw0</ref>
