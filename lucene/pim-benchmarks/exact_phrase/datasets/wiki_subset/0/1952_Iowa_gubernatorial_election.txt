The '''1952 Iowa gubernatorial election''' was held on November 4, 1952. Incumbent [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] [[William S. Beardsley]] defeated [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic]] nominee [[Herschel C. Loveless]] with 51.90% of the vote.

==Primary elections==
[[Primary election]]s were held on June 2, 1952.<ref name="Iowa">{{cite web|url=https://sos.iowa.gov/elections/pdf/results/50s/1952primcanv.pdf |title=Summary of Official Canvass of Votes Cast in Iowa Primary Election |publisher=[[Secretary of State of Iowa]] |year=1952 |accessdate=April 3, 2020}}</ref>

===Democratic primary===

====Candidates====
*[[Herschel C. Loveless]], [[Ottumwa, Iowa|Mayor of Ottumwa]]
*[[Otha Wearin]], former [[United States House of Representatives|U.S. Representative]]
