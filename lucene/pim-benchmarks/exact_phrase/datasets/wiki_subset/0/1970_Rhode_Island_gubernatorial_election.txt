The '''1970 Rhode Island gubernatorial election''' was held on November 3, 1970. Incumbent [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democrat]] [[Frank Licht]] defeated [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] nominee [[Herbert F. DeSimone]] with 50.07% of the vote.

==Primary elections==
[[Primary election]]s were held on September 15, 1970.<ref name="Rhode Island">{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9CZECwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1802 |title=Guide to U.S. Elections |isbn=9781483380353 |accessdate=April 3, 2020|last1=Kalb |first1=Deborah |date=24 December 2015 }}</ref>
