'''Marjolaine Hecquet''' (born 25 March 1993) is a French female [[Canoeing|canoeist]] who won five medals at individual senior level at the [[Wildwater Canoeing World Championships]] and [[European Wildwater Championships]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.canoeresults.eu/view-results/wildwater?eventid[]=108#event|title=World and European Championships Results from 1959 to 2019|publisher=canoeresults.eu|accessdate=3 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.canoeicf.com/sites/default/files/results_history_-_complete_medal_winners_icf_updated_2007.pdf|title=INTERNATIONAL CANOE FEDERATION MEDAL WINNERS - Olympic Games and World Championships (1959-2008)|publisher=canoeicf.com|accessdate=3 April 2020}}</ref>

==Biography==
Hecquet practiced the [[canoe sprint]] up to the [[under-23]] level and then moved definitively to the [[wildwater canoeing]] where she obtained the best results, including three world champion titles.
