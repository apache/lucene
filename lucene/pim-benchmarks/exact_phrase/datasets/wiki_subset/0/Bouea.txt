'''''Bouea'''''<ref> Meisner CDF (1837) ''Pl. Vasc. Gen.'' 1: 75.</ref> is an Asian genus of fruiting trees in the family [[Anacardiaceae]].<ref name = POWO>[http://www.plantsoftheworldonline.org/taxon/urn:lsid:ipni.org:names:1754-1 Plants of the World Online (POWO): ''Bouea'' Meisn. (retrieved 3 April 2020)]</ref><ref name = "COL"/>  Species can be found in southern China, [[Indo-China]] and [[Malesia]].<ref name = POWO/>

== Species ==
[[Plants of the World Online]]<ref name = POWO/> and the [[Catalogue of Life]]<ref name = "COL"/> list:
* ''[[Bouea macrophylla]]'' [[Griff.]] (Thailand, Peninsular Malaysia, Sumatra, Java)
* ''[[Bouea oppositifolia]]'' ([[Roxb.]]) [[Meisn.]] - [[type species]] (throughout range)
* ''[[Bouea poilanei]]'' [[Charles Marie Evrard|Evrard]] (Vietnam only)
