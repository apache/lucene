She has also presided the Chikkaballapura District Kannada Sahitya Sammelana, held in Shidlaghatta in the year 2013.

==Awards and honours==
Gudibande poornima has won many awards like, [[Sahitya Akademi Award]], Shri Gomateshwara Vidyapeetha award, Mallika award, Sharada Seva Shree, etc.{{citation needed|date=January 2013}}

The Karanataka Lekhakiyara Sangha, an organization and association of women writers has established a yearly award in her name which is called as "The Gudibande Poornima awards for poets" is given to prominent [[Kannada language|Kannada]] Women poets every year.<ref>[http://www.hindu.com/2004/05/17/stories/2004051711800500.htm The Hindu : Karnataka News : Anupama Award presented to Sunandamma<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
