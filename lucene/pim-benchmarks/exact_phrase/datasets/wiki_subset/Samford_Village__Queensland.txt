'''Samford Village''' is a [[Suburbs and localities (Australia)|locality]] in the [[Moreton Bay Region]], [[Queensland]], Australia.<ref name=qpnl>{{cite QPN|45469|Samford Village|locality in Moreton Bay Region|accessdate=28 October 2017}}</ref>

== Community groups ==
The Samford branch of the [[Queensland Country Women's Association]] meets at the QCWA Hall at 31 Main Street.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.qcwa.org.au/branch-locations/|title=Branch Locations|last=|first=|date=|website=|publisher=[[Queensland Country Women's Association]]|access-date=26 December 2018}}</ref>

The Samford Support Network is a group of volunteers providing a variety of services to community members who are ill, elderly, living with a disability, or struggling.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.australianoftheyear.org.au/honour-roll/?view=fullView&recipientID=2087&year=1960|title=2019 QLD Local Hero Nominee: Ms Barbara Kienast|last=|first=|date=2019|website=[[Australian of the Year Awards]]|access-date=2019-10-22}}</ref>
