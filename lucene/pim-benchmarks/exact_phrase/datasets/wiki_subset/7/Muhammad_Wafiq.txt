'''Muhammad Wafiq''' (born 25 April 1996) is a Malaysian [[cricket]]er.<ref name="Bio">{{Cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/player/568452.html |title=Muhammad Wafiq |accessdate=23 May 2017 |work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref> He played for [[Malaysia national cricket team|Malaysia]] in the [[2017 ICC World Cricket League Division Three]] tournament in May 2017.<ref name="Div3">{{Cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/match/1090938.html |title=ICC World Cricket League Division Three, Malaysia v Singapore at Kampala, May 23, 2017 |accessdate=23 May 2017 |work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref> In April 2018, he was named in Malaysia's squad for the [[2018 ICC World Cricket League Division Four]] tournament, also in Malaysia.<ref name="MasSquad">{{cite web|url=https://www.icc-cricket.com/media-releases/673100 |title=Six teams travel to Malaysia on road to ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 |date=28 April 2018 |accessdate=28 April 2018 |work=International Cricket Council}}</ref> He was named as the player to watch in the squad ahead of the tournament.<ref name="watch">{{cite web |url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/story/_/id/23342609/groundwork-2023-world-cup-begins-wcl-division-four |title=Groundwork for 2023 World Cup begins at WCL Division Four |accessdate=28 April 2018 |work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref> In October 2018, he was named in Malaysia's squad in the Eastern sub-region group for the [[2018–19 ICC World Twenty20 Asia Qualifier]] tournament.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.icc-cricket.com/media-releases/869213 |title=Exciting battle on the cards in the ICC World T20 Asia Qualifier B in Malaysia |work=International Cricket Council |accessdate=2 October 2018}}</ref>

In September 2019, he was named in Malaysia's [[Twenty20 International]] (T20I) squad for their series [[Vanuatuan cricket team in Malaysia in 2019–20|against Vanuatu]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cricketmalaysia.com/MalaysianCricketAssociation/viewTeam.do?teamId=1951&clubId=10933 |title=Vanuatu Squad |work=Malaysian Cricket Association |accessdate=28 September 2019}}</ref> He made his T20I debut for Malayasia, against [[Vanuatu national cricket team|Vanuatu]], on 29 September 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/match/1202007.html |title=1st T20I, Vanuatu tour of Malaysia at Kuala Lumpur, Sep 29 2019 |work=ESPN Cricinfo |accessdate=29 September 2019}}</ref>
