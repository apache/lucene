'''Mohamad Fikri Makram Rosdi''' (born 29 June 1993), known as '''Fikri Makram''', is a Malaysian [[cricket]]er.<ref name="Bio">{{Cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/content/player/1037553.html |title=Fikri Makram |accessdate=23 May 2017 |work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref> He played for [[Malaysia national cricket team|Malaysia]] in the [[2014 ICC World Cricket League Division Five]] tournament in March 2014,<ref name="DivFive">{{Cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/wcldiv5-2014/engine/match/722655.html |title=ICC World Cricket League Division Five, Malaysia v Tanzania at Kuala Lumpur, Mar 6, 2014 |accessdate=19 July 2015 |work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref> and in the [[2017 ICC World Cricket League Division Three]] tournament in May 2017.<ref name="Div3">{{Cite web|url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/ci/engine/match/1090938.html |title=ICC World Cricket League Division Three, Malaysia v Singapore at Kampala, May 23, 2017 |accessdate=23 May 2017 |work=ESPN Cricinfo}}</ref>
