'''''Wonder Woman: Original Motion Picture Soundtrack''''' is the [[soundtrack]] to the [[Wonder Woman (2017 film)|film of the same name]]. 
The music is composed and arranged by [[Rupert Gregson-Williams]]. It was released on June 2, 2017, by [[WaterTower Music]].

On November 3, 2016, [[Rupert Gregson-Williams]] was hired to compose the film's music.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://screenrant.com/wonder-woman-composer-hacksaw-ridge-rupert-gregson-williams/|title=Wonder Woman Gets Hacksaw Ridge Composer|date=2016-11-03|work=Screen Rant|access-date=2017-04-28|language=en-US}}</ref><ref name="Wonder Woman Gets Hacksaw Ridge Composer">{{cite web|title=Wonder Woman Gets Hacksaw Ridge Composer|url=http://screenrant.com/wonder-woman-composer-hacksaw-ridge-rupert-gregson-williams/|publisher=Screen Rant|accessdate=November 4, 2016}}</ref> He is joined by Evan Jolly, Tom Howe,<ref name="tomhowe">{{cite web|title=Credits: Tom Howe|url=http://www.tomhowe.co.uk/credits/|publisher=tomhowe.co.uk|accessdate=March 25, 2017}}</ref> Paul Mounsey,<ref name="paulmounsey">{{cite web|title=Credits: Paul Mounsey|url=http://www.hans-zimmer.com/index.php?rub=rcpmember&numid=122|publisher=hans-zimmer.com|accessdate=May 2, 2017}}</ref> and Andrew Kawczynski<ref name="andrewkawczynski">{{cite web|title=Credits: Andrew Kawczynski|url=http://www.hans-zimmer.com/index.php?rub=rcpmember&numid=115|publisher=hans-zimmer.com|accessdate=May 19, 2017}}</ref> who provide additional music. The soundtrack was released on the same day as the film on CD, digital, and vinyl.<ref name="RupertGWilliams">{{cite web|title=Rupert Gregson-Williams|url=https://twitter.com/RupertGWilliams/status/860081860944293888|publisher=@RupertGWilliams on Twitter|accessdate=May 19, 2017}}</ref> The score also heavily samples the "Is She with You?" theme from the film ''[[Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice]]'', which was written by [[Hans Zimmer]] and [[Junkie XL]].

Australian singer [[Sia (musician)|Sia]] sang a song for the film, titled "[[To Be Human]]", featuring English musician [[Labrinth]]. Written by [[Florence Welch]] and [[Rick Nowels]], the song is featured on the soundtrack.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://radio.com/2017/05/11/sia-new-track-to-be-human-wonder-woman/|title=Sia's New Track, 'To Be Human,' Featured on 'Wonder Woman' Soundtrack|website=radio.com|accessdate=1 June 2017}}</ref> The single was released on May 25, 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/pop/7809337/sia-wonder-woman-song-to-be-human-listen|title=Sia Channels Her Inner 'Wonder Woman' with New 'To Be Human' Single for Soundtrack: Listen|publisher=Billboard|accessdate=June 4, 2017}}</ref>
