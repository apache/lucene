'''Dominie Pittman''' (born October 13, 1986) is a [[defensive end]] who is currently a free agent. He has also played for the [[BC Lions]] of the [[Canadian Football League]]. Pittman signed as a [[free agent]] with the Lions on April 22, 2010.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://bclions.com/article/lions-add-linebacker-and-d-line-depth |title=Lions add linebacker and D-Line depth |date=April 22, 2010 |publisher=British Columbia Lions |work=www.bclions.com |accessdate=July 3, 2010}}</ref> He played [[college football]] for the [[North Alabama Lions football|North Alabama Lions]].

On June 6, 2015, Pittman was traded by the [[Los Angeles KISS]] to the [[New Orleans VooDoo]] to complete the trade of [[Rayshaun Kizer]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.amitetoday.com/sports-college-sports/veteran-lineman-dominie-pittman-joins-voodoo |title=Veteran Lineman Dominie Pittman Joins VooDoo |author=Virgil Howell |date=June 25, 2015 |publisher=Amite-Tangi Digest |work=www.tangilena.com |accessdate=July 2, 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150704084249/http://www.amitetoday.com/sports-college-sports/veteran-lineman-dominie-pittman-joins-voodoo |archive-date=July 4, 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

On January 28, 2016, Pittman was assigned to the [[Tampa Bay Storm]].
