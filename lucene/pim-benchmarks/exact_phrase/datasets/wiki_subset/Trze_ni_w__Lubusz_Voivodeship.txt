'''Trześniów''' {{IPAc-pl|'|t|sz|e|ś|ń|u|f}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Ośno Lubuskie]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|7|km|mi|0}} north-east of [[Ośno Lubuskie]], {{convert|31|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of [[Słubice]], {{convert|35|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and {{convert|71|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of Zielona Góra.

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').

The village has a population of 170.
