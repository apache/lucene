'''''Eudociminus''''' is a genus of [[pine weevil]]s in the [[beetle]] family [[Curculionidae]]. There are at least two described species in ''Eudociminus''.<ref name=itis/><ref name=gbif/><ref name=buglink/>

==Species==
These two species belong to the genus ''Eudociminus'':
* ''[[Eudociminus mannerheimi]]'' <small>Boheman, 1836</small>
* ''[[Eudociminus mannerheimii]]'' <small>(Boheman, 1936)</small> (cypress weevil)
