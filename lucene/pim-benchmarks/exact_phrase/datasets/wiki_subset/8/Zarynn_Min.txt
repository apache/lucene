'''Zarynn Min''' (born '''Noraszarinawati binti Tumin'''; 7 December 1980) is a Malaysian actress.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mstar.com.my/hiburan/berita-hiburan/2010/04/14/proses-bercerai-lebih-mahal-dari-proses-berkahwin--zaryn-min/|title=Proses Bercerai Lebih Mahal Dari Proses Berkahwin - Zaryn Min - Berita Hiburan {{!}} mStar|last=AZIZ|first=SITI AZIRA ABD.|website=www.mstar.com.my|access-date=2018-05-02|date=14 April 2010}}</ref>

==Early career==
Min graduated in Broadcasting from Akademi TV3 and Business Studies from [[Stamford, Texas|Stamford College]]. Her first TV appearance was in ''Rafflesia'' as Hidayah for [[Radio Televisyen Malaysia|RTM]] (awarded Best TV Series, Anugerah Skrin 2006). Since then, she has acted in numerous TV series and tele-movies including ''Salam Terakhir'', alongside Malaysian award-winning and popular actor, Datoâ€™ Rosyam Nor. She debuted in ''Anak Mami: The Movie'', followed by ''[[Dunia Baru The Movie|Dunia Baru: The Movie]]'', ''Jin'' and ''Suamiku Mr. Perfect 10.''

In 2010, Min appeared as a leading actress in ''Aalia'' (68 episodes for ''[[Astro Prima]])'' as well as her first psychological thriller [[Television film|telefilm]] ''Gila''.

Additional credit, she holds a Black Belt First Dan from Malaysia International Tae Kwon Do Federation at the age of 12 : has won a Silver and two Gold Medals through out her active years.

She has an IQ score of 130

==Nominations==
* In 2011 she was nominated ''Best Actress In A Leading Role'' by the [[Asian Television Awards|16th Asian Television Awards]] for her performance in ''Gila''.
* In 2015, she was nominated for Best Supporting Actress in ''Anugerah Tribute P.Ramlee'' for her performance in ''Menunggu Hujan Teduh''
* In 2016 she was nominated for the same category for her performance in ''Aku Dan Dia''.
* She received her first awards at ANUGERAH TELENOVELA 2018 for BEST SUPPORTING ACTRESS IN TELENOVELA  held in Plenary Hall KLCC.
