'''''Nannaria''''' is a genus of [[flat-backed millipede]]s in the family [[Xystodesmidae]]. There are more than 20 described species in ''Nannaria''.<ref name=itis/><ref name=gbif/><ref name=buglink/><ref name=Shelley/>

==Species==
These 27 species belong to the genus ''Nannaria'':

* ''[[Nannaria austricola]]'' <small>Hoffman, 1950</small>
* ''[[Nannaria castanea]]'' <small>(McNeill, 1887)</small>
* ''[[Nannaria cayugae]]'' <small>Chamberlin, 1949</small>
* ''[[Nannaria conservata]]'' <small>Chamberlin, 1940</small>
* ''[[Nannaria davidcauseyi]]'' <small>Causey, 1950</small>
* ''[[Nannaria depalmai]]'' <small>(Causey, 1950)</small>
* ''[[Nannaria domestica]]'' <small>Shelley, 1975</small>
* ''[[Nannaria equalis]]'' <small>Chamberlin, 1949</small>
* ''[[Nannaria ericacea]]'' <small>Hoffman, 1949</small>
* ''[[Nannaria fowleri]]'' <small>Chamberlin, 1947</small>
