'''''Cryptocephalus mutabilis''''' is a species of case-bearing [[leaf beetle]] in the family [[Chrysomelidae]].<ref name=itis/><ref name=catlife/><ref name=gbif/><ref name=bugref/> It is found in North America.<ref name=itis/><ref name=xcit1/>
