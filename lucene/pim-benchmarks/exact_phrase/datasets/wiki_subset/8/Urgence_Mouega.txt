'''Urgence Mouega''' (born 16 November 1994) is a [[taekwondo]] practitioner from Gabon who won a gold medal in the women's lightweight at the [[2011 All-Africa Games]]. In the same event at the [[2015 African Games]] she finished with a bronze medal. She has competed in two editions of the [[World Taekwondo Championships]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.taekwondodata.com/urgence-mouega.agxd.html|title=Mouega, Urgence|publisher=Taekwondo Data|accessdate=25 April 2018}}</ref> At the [[2016 African Taekwondo Olympic Qualification Tournament]] she was a semi-final win away from qualifying for the Rio Olympics.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://dailynewsegypt.com/2016/02/11/three-egyptian-taekwondo-players-qualify-for-2016-rio-de-janeiro-summer-olympics/|title=Three Egyptian taekwondo players qualify for 2016 Rio de Janeiro Summer Olympics|work=[[Daily News Egypt]]|accessdate=25 April 2018}}</ref>
