'''Stadio Bruno Recchioni''' is a multi-use [[stadium]] in [[Fermo]], [[Italy]].  It is used mostly for [[football (soccer)|football]] matches and is the home ground of [[Fermana Calcio]].  The stadium holds 9,500.
