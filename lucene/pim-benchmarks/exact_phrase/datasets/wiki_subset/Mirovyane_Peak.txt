'''Mirovyane Peak''' ({{lang-bg|връх Мировяне|vrah Mirovyane}}, {{IPA-bg|ˈvrɤx miˈrɔvʲɐnɛ|IPA}}) is the peak rising to 1500 m in [[Maglenik Heights]], north-central [[Sentinel Range]] in [[Ellsworth Mountains]], [[Antarctica]].  It is surmounting [[Kopsis Glacier]] to the west, lower [[Embree Glacier]] to the north, and [[Young Glacier]] to the south.

The peak is named after the settlement of Mirovyane in Western Bulgaria.

==Location==
Mirovyane Peak is located at {{coord|78|04|30|S|85|04|54|W|display=inline,title}}, which is 3.46&nbsp;km northeast of [[Zimornitsa Peak]], 13.68&nbsp;km southeast of [[Mount Schmid]] and 17.94&nbsp;km north-northwest of [[Mount Besch]].  US mapping in 1961, updated in 1988.
