'''Joe Brooks''' was a [[association football|footballer]] from [[Stalybridge]], England. He played in the [[Football League]] for [[Glossop North End A.F.C.|Glossop]] and [[Sheffield United F.C.|Sheffield United]], and the [[Southern Football League|Southern League]] for [[Watford F.C.|Watford]] and [[Stalybridge Celtic F.C.|Stalybridge Celtic]]. Early in his career, Brooks also played for his hometown's previous club, [[Stalybridge Rovers F.C.|Stalybridge Rovers]] prior to its dissolution.<ref name=WhosWho/><ref name=sbs/>
