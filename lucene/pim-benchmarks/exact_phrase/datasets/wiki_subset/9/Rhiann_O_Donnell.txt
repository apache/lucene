'''Rhiann Kate O'Donnell''' (born 14 April 1998 in [[Mildura]], [[Victoria (Australia)|Victoria]]) is an [[Australians|Australian]] [[cricket]] player.<ref name="ESPNcricinfo - Rhiann O'Donnell - Australia">{{cite web |title=Rhiann O'Donnell - Australia |url=http://www.espncricinfo.com/australia/content/player/1128754.html |website=[[ESPNcricinfo]] |publisher=[[ESPN Inc.]] |accessdate=6 April 2019}}</ref> She played her first [[Women's National Cricket League]] match for the [[Victoria women's cricket team]] in its [[2018–19 Women's National Cricket League season|2018–19 season]].<ref name="Cricket Australia - Rhiann O'Donnell">{{cite web |title=Rhiann O'Donnell |url=https://www.cricket.com.au/players/rhiann-odonnell/ |website=[[Cricket Australia]] |accessdate=6 April 2019}}</ref> 

O'Donnell played for the [[Melbourne Renegades (WBBL)|Melbourne Renegades]] in [[2017–18 Women's Big Bash League season|WBBL|03]]<ref name="Melbourne Renegades -  Rhiann O'Donnell">{{cite web |title=Melbourne Renegades - Rhiann O'Donnell |url=https://www.melbournerenegades.com.au/players/rhiann-odonnell |publisher=[[Melbourne Renegades]] |accessdate=6 April 2019}}</ref> She played for the [[Hobart Hurricanes (WBBL)|Hobart Hurricanes]] in  [[2018–19 Women's Big Bash League season|WBBL|04]]<ref name="The Examiner - WBBL -: Hurricanes sign Rhiann O'Donnell for 2018-19 season">{{cite news |last1=Geale |first1=Hamiash |title=WBBL: Hurricanes sign Rhiann O'Donnell for 2018-19 season |url=https://www.examiner.com.au/story/5763136/hurricanes-snap-up-young-victorian/ |accessdate=6 April 2019 |work=[[The Examiner (Tasmania)]] |date=17 November 2018}}</ref>
