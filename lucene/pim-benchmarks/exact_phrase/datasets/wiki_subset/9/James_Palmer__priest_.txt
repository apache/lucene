'''James Palmer''' (died 1660) was a priest and philanthropist who is remembered in a bust at [[Westminster Abbey]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.westminster-abbey.org/abbey-commemorations/commemorations/james-palmer|title=James Palmer|first=PixelToCode|last=pixeltocode.uk|website=Westminster Abbey|accessdate=August 18, 2019}}</ref> and in the name of [[Palmer Street]]. In 1655 he founded what became known as Palmer's Village in London.<ref>Bebbington, Gillian. (1972) ''London Street Names''. London: B.T. Batsford. p. 246. {{ISBN|0713401400}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.londonremembers.com/subjects/reverend-james-palmer/|title=Reverend James Palmer|website=London Remembers|accessdate=August 18, 2019}}</ref>
