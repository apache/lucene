'''''Masa podrida''''' are traditional [[Filipino cuisine|Filipino]] [[shortbread cookie]]s made from flour, salt, baking powder, brown sugar, shortening, and eggs. It has a dry crumbly texture similar to [[Half-moon cookie (Filipino)|half-moon cookie]]s. The name comes from [[Spanish language|Spanish]] for "rotten flour".
