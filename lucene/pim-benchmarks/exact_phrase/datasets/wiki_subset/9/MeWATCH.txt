'''meWATCH''' is a Singaporean digital [[video on demand]] service brand owned by [[Mediacorp]]. It was launched on 1 February 2013 as an [[Over-the-top media services|OTT]] service, and as an entertainment and lifestyle website under its original name '''Toggle'''.

On 1 April 2015, [[xinmsn]] was closed down and merged with Toggle.<ref>{{cite web|first1=Kyle Malinda|last1=Shah|title=Goodbye Xinmsn, Hello Toggle: Microsoft & MediaCorp Disband Entertainment Site|url=https://sg.news.yahoo.com/goodbye-xinmsn-hello-toggle-microsoft-014111654.html|website=[[Yahoo]]|accessdate=15 November 2016|date=1 April 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=TOGGLE|url=https://www11.mediacorp.sg/explore/list/toggle|website=Mediacorp|accessdate=15 November 2016|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20161115133647/https://www11.mediacorp.sg/explore/list/toggle|archivedate=15 November 2016|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|first1=Ryan|last1=Lawler|title=MediaCorp Taps Tvinci To Launch Toggle, Its Virtual Cable Service In Singapore|url=https://techcrunch.com/2013/01/28/mediacorp-tvinci-toggle/|website=[[TechCrunch]]|accessdate=15 November 2016|date=28 January 2013}}</ref>

==Content==
[[File:Toggle logo (2013).jpg|thumb|right|150px|Toggle first logo (2013-2015)]]
[[File:Toggle logo.svg|thumb|right|150px|Toggle second and last logo (2015-2020)]]
meWATCH offers to worldwide audiences video streaming or on-demand content of programs from Mediacorp's archived library as well as original webseries. In addition, meWatch also offers [[live streaming]] of Mediacorp's free-to-air channels (exclusive to Singapore only). It also offers catch-up TV for viewers to watch shows they have missed on prime time TV shows from the previous few days.

In 2016, meWATCH (then Toggle) began to offer made-for-digital productions under the brand Toggle Originals.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.asiantvawards.com/2016/02/toggle-originals-as-a-differentiating-factor/|title=Toggle Originals as a differentiating factor|date=2016-02-12|website=Asian Television Awards|language=en-US|access-date=2019-08-14}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.todayonline.com/entertainment/television/toggle-launch-11-new-shows|title=Toggle to launch 11 new shows|website=TODAYonline|access-date=2019-08-14}}</ref> Certain original series, such as ''[[I Want to Be a Star]]'' may be telecasted on Mediacorp's television channels, typically in the late night programming slots.<ref>{{cite web| url= https://123movies247.com/ |title= Movies123 Guide }} Monday, July 13, 2020 </ref>

In 2019, meWATCH began offering additional content from [[HBO Go]] for Singapore residents only.<ref>{{cite web|first1=Douglas|last1=Tseng|title=Viewers Can Now Watch 'Game Of Thrones' On Toggle With HBO Go|url=https://www.8days.sg/seeanddo/tvreviews/viewers-can-now-watch-game-of-thrones-on-toggle-with-hbo-go-11234784|website=[[8 Days (magazine)|8 Days]]|accessdate=6 April 2019|date=13 February 2019}}</ref> In the same year, Mediacorp and Wattpad inked partnership for developing scripted series and films for meWATCH and Mediacorp FTA channels, based on books made by Wattpad writers that are based in Singapore.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://company.wattpad.com/blog/2019/4/23/wattpad-and-mediacorp-partner-to-bring-singapore-stories-from-written-word-to-national-screens|title=Wattpad and Mediacorp partner to bring Singapore stories from written word to national screens|website=Wattpad HQ|language=en-US|access-date=2019-12-09}}</ref> In 30 May 2019, meWATCH began offering on-demand Korean movies from [[TVN (South Korean TV channel)|tvN Movies]].<ref>{{cite web|first1=Douglas|last1=Tseng|title=Toggle Viewers Can Now Stream Korean Blockbusters With tvN Movies|url=https://www.todayonline.com/8days/seeanddo/toggle-viewers-can-now-stream-korean-blockbusters-tvn-movies|website=[[Today (Singapore newspaper)|Today]]|accessdate=30 January 2020|date=30 May 2019}}</ref>

On 30 January 2020, Toggle was renamed to meWATCH.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.marketing-interactive.com/mediacorp-revamps-toggle-meradio-and-meclub/|title=Mediacorp revamps Toggle, MeRadio and MeClub|last=Farveen|first=Farzanah|date=2019-11-04|website=Marketing Interactive|language=en-US|access-date=2019-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Mediacorp rolls out meWATCH, meLISTEN, meREWARDS and meCONNECT |url=https://www.mediacorp.sg/en/mediacorp-in-the-news/media-releases/mediacorp-rolls-out-mewatch-melisten-merewards-and-meconnect-12369778 |website=Mediacorp |accessdate=30 January 2020 |date=30 January 2020}}</ref> At the same time, Mediacorp made a content deal with [[HOOQ]] to stream the latter's original content.<ref>{{cite web|first1=Stephen|last1=Netto|title=Mediacorp clinches content and subscription deals with HOOQ to champion cross-company content to audiences across Asia|url=https://www.theonlinecitizen.com/2020/01/30/mediacorp-clinches-content-and-subscription-deals-with-hooq-to-champion-cross-company-content-to-audiences-across-asia/|website=The Online Citizen|accessdate=30 January 2020|date=30 January 2020}}</ref> However, on 27 March 2020, HOOQ filed for liquidation, and shut down on 30 April 2020.<ref>{{Cite news|date=2020-03-27|title=Singtel-backed streaming service HOOQ files for liquidation|language=en|work=Reuters|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-singtel-hooq-idUSKBN21E0QS|access-date=2020-05-28}}</ref> HOOQ eventually was acquired by [[Coupang]] in July 2020.
