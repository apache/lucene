'''''Sadbhav'' Engineering Limited''' (SEL) is a [[civil engineering]] [[construction]] company based in [[Ahmedabad]], [[Gujarat]], [[India]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sadbhaveng.com/History.htm |title=Welcome To Sadbhav Engineering Ltd |publisher=Sadbhaveng.com |date= |accessdate=2010-08-09}}</ref> It was founded in 1988 by Vishnubhai M. Patel. Sadbhav has implemented projects in Construction of Roads & Highways, Bridges, Mining and Irrigation supporting infrastructure. Sadbhav Engineering Ltd is listed on [[BSE SENSEX|BSE]] and [[National Stock Exchange of India|NSE]]. The company worked for clients including [[National Highways Authority of India|NHAI]], Sardar Sarovar Narmada Nigam, [[Coal India]], GIPCL, GHCL, L&T, HCC, [[Punj Lloyd]] among various others.<ref name=sel-profile>{{cite web|url=https://www.sadbhaveng.com/about-us/|title=Welcome To Sadbhav Engineering Ltd |publisher=Sadbhaveng.com |date=1989-10-01 |accessdate=2019-04-08}}</ref> 

SEL was listed on the [[Bombay Stock Exchange]] and the [[National Stock Exchange of India]] in 2001. As on 31 March 2019, market capitalisation of the company stood at [[Indian rupee|₹]]4,274 [[crore]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.moneycontrol.com/india/stockpricequote/infrastructure-general/sadbhavengineering/SE19|title=Sadbhav Engineering Ltd Share Price Live, Sadbhav Engineering Stock Price Today|website=Moneycontrol}}</ref>
