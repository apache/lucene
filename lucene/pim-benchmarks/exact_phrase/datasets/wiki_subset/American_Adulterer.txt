'''''American Adulterer''''' is a 2009 novel written by [[Jed Mercurio]] focusing on the life of 35th President of the United States [[John Fitzgerald Kennedy]]. It mainly talks about his [[extramarital affairs]], political ambitions, various physical ailments and his relationship with his wife [[Jackie Kennedy Onassis|Jackie]] and his children.
