'''Świniary''' {{IPAc-pl|ś|f|i|'|ń|a|r|y}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Ośno Lubuskie]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> 

It lies approximately {{convert|5|km|mi|0}} south-west of [[Ośno Lubuskie]], {{convert|20|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of [[Słubice]], {{convert|44|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and {{convert|73|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of Zielona Góra.

The village has a population of 220.
