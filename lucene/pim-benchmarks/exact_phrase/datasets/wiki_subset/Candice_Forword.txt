'''Candice {{sic|hide=y|For|word}}''' (born 19 April 1979) is a [[field hockey]] forward from [[South Africa]], who did not make it into the national squad that finished 9th at the [[2004 Summer Olympics]] in [[Athens]]. The striker made her international debut in 2003. {{sic|hide=y|For|word}} comes from [[Pretoria]], and is [[nickname]]d ''Cands''. She plays for a provincial team called Northern Gauteng. She announced her retirement from international hockey on 12 March 2007.<ref>[http://www.nbha.co.za/news.htm Northern "Blues" Hockey Association News - News from 12 March 2007] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070928120255/http://www.nbha.co.za/news.htm |date=28 September 2007 }} Retrieved 22 August 2007</ref>

==Education==
{{sic|hide=y|For|word}} attended Stirling Primary<ref>[http://www.dispatch.co.za/2001/09/22/sport/CHOCKEY.HTM Dispatch Online - ''Bright sporting future assured for hockey star'' written by Peter Martin, 22 September 2001] Retrieved 22 August 2007</ref> and [[Clarendon High School for Girls]].<ref>[http://www.go.co.za/2003/12/04/features/HIGHSCHO.HTM Go and Express - ''An institution and a statement'' written by Rob Sampson, 4 December 2003] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061014092421/http://www.go.co.za/2003/12/04/features/HIGHSCHO.HTM |date=14 October 2006 }} Retrieved 20 August 2007</ref> She studied marketing at [[Durban University of Technology|Natal Technikon]], and went on to the [[University of Pretoria]].<ref>[http://www.up.ac.za/sport/sportclubs/hockey/index.php University of Pretoria - Hockey] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050910085142/http://www.up.ac.za/sport/sportclubs/hockey/index.php |date=10 September 2005 }} Retrieved 22 August 2007</ref>

==Personal==
Candice's mother, Beverly, played six times for the South Africa hockey team. Her sister, Danielle, was a member of the South African Junior World Cup team in 2005, and a member of the Under-18 team in 2005, and captain of it in 2006.<ref>[http://tarheelblue.cstv.com/sports/w-fieldh/mtt/forword_danielle00.html Tar Heel Athletics - Profile of Danielle Forword] Retrieved 22 August 2007</ref>
