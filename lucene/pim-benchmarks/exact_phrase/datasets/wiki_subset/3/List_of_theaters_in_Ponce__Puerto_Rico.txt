This '''list of theaters in Ponce, Puerto Rico''', consists of both [[Movie theater|movie]] theaters as well as the traditional [[performing arts]] theaters in the city of [[Ponce, Puerto Rico]]. Both historical (including no longer existing) as well a currently operating theaters are listed.  During the first half of the 20th century, most theaters have been located in the [[Zona Historica de Ponce|central urban zone]] of the city. One prominent exception is Teatro Miramar, the theater that served the Playa community in barrio [[Playa (Ponce)|Playa]], Ponce. Today's cinema theaters oftentimes have several screens under a single roof.
