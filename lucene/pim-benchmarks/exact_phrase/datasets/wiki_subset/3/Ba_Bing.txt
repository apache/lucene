"'''Ba*Bing'''" is a song performed by Australian [[Disk jockey|DJ]] and recording artist [[Havana Brown (musician)|Havana Brown]] from her debut studio album ''[[Flashing Lights (album)|Flashing Lights]]'' (2013). It was released as a [[promotional single]] from the album on 13 September 2013. The single was released on 6 November 2015 as the third single from ''Flashing Lights''.<ref name="Ba Bing - single by Havana Brown">{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/au/album/ba*bing-single/id1050557841|title=Ba Bing - single by Havana Brown|publisher=iTunes Australia|accessdate=8 February 2017}}</ref>

The recording, which takes its name from Brown's dog, was written by Brown, Luciana Caporaso, Carl Ryden and Nick Clow, with Clow producing the track. "Ba*Bing" debuted, and peaked, at number forty-six on the Australian [[ARIA Charts|ARIA Singles Charts]] in 2013.

==Background and reception==
"Ba*Bing" was written by Havana Brown, together with [[Luciana Caporaso]], Nick Clow and Carl Ryden, with Clow handling production.<ref name="apra">{{cite web|url=http://www.apra-amcos.com.au/worksearch.axd?q=Ba*Bing |title=Ba*Bing |publisher=[[Australasian Performing Right Association]] |accessdate=8 May 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140508113250/http://www.apra-amcos.com.au/worksearch.axd?q=Ba%2ABing |archivedate=8 May 2014 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/song/babing-mt0048008338|title=Ba*Bing - Havana Brown|publisher=[[AllMusic]] ([[All Media Network]])|date=|accessdate=7 May 2014}}</ref> The track, which Brown notes as one of her favourites,<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.flamingopink.com.au/2013/10/quickie-with-dj-havana-brown/ |title=Quickie with Havana Brown |last=Harriet |first= |publisher=Flamingo Pink |date=11 October 2013 |accessdate=7 May 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140508101135/http://www.flamingopink.com.au/2013/10/quickie-with-dj-havana-brown/ |archivedate=8 May 2014 }}</ref> is [[Eponym|named after]] Brown's [[Yorkshire Terrier|yorkshire terrier]], who would often be in the studio with her during recording sessions.<ref name="Official">{{cite web|url=http://www.havanabrownmusic.com/about/|title=Havana Brown - About|last=|publisher=Havana Brown Official Site|accessdate=8 May 2014| archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/query?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.havanabrownmusic.com%2Fabout%2F&date=2014-05-08| archivedate= 8 May 2014 <!--DASHBot-->| url-status= live}}</ref> The song, which incorporates a [[Bhangra (music)|bhangra]] beat,<ref name="Official" /> was described by Mike Wass from [[Idolator (website)|Idolator]] as "sexy" and "spin bone-rattling", and a writer for [[Take40 Australia]] called it "a quirky dance track".<ref name="idolator" /><ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.take40.com/artists/4469/havana-brown/news/43172/review:-havana-brown-melbourne-concert---trak-lounge-02.11.13 |title=REVIEW: Havana Brown Melbourne Concert - Trak Lounge 02.11.13 |last= |first= |publisher=[[Take 40 Australia]] (MCM Entertainment, Pty Ltd.) |date=4 November 2013 |accessdate=7 May 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140508145858/http://www.take40.com/artists/4469/havana-brown/news/43172/review%3A-havana-brown-melbourne-concert---trak-lounge-02.11.13 |archivedate=8 May 2014 }}</ref>

"Ba*Bing" was released digitally on 13 September 2013, as a [[promotional single]]<ref name="idolator">{{cite news|url=http://www.idolator.com/7483401/havana-brown-flashing-lights-cover-tracklist-ba-bing |title=Havana Brown Unveils ‘Flashing Lights’ Cover & Tracklist, Drops Sexy Buzz Track "Ba*Bing" |last=Wass|first=Mike|publisher=[[Idolator (website)|Idolator]] ([[Spin Media]]) |date=9 September 2013 |accessdate=7 May 2014}}</ref> from her debut album ''[[Flashing Lights (album)|Flashing Lights]]'' (2013).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://itunes.apple.com/au/album/ba*bing/id699086683?i=699087297|title=Flashing Lights (Deluxe Version) - Havana Brown|work=|publisher=[[iTunes Store]] (Australia) [[Apple Inc.|Apple]]|accessdate=3 October 2013}}</ref> "Ba*Bing" debuted on the Australian [[ARIA Charts|ARIA Singles Chart]] at number forty-six on 24 September 2013, becoming her third song to chart within the top one hundred.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ariacharts.com.au/news/42439/chartifacts---tuesday-24th-september,-2013 |title=Chartifacts - Tuesday 24th September, 2013 |publisher=[[ARIA Charts]] ([[Australian Record Industry Association]] (ARIA)) |accessdate=7 May 2014 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140508101418/http://www.ariacharts.com.au/news/42439/chartifacts---tuesday-24th-september%2C-2013 |archivedate=8 May 2014 }}</ref> The recording only spent one week on the charts.<ref name="singlescharts">{{cite web|url=http://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Havana+Brown&titel=Ba%2ABing&cat=s |title=HAVANA BROWN - BA*BING (SONG) |publisher=australian - charts.com |accessdate=3 October 2013 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131013210255/http://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Havana+Brown&titel=Ba%2ABing&cat=s |archivedate=13 October 2013 }}</ref> "Ba*Bing" also appeared on the ARIA Dance Charts, spending two weeks in the top twenty, and peaking at number nine.<ref name="ausdnccharts">{{cite web|url=http://pandora.nla.gov.au/pan/23790/20130924-1506/Issue1230.pdf|title=ARIA Charts - Issue#1230 - Week of 23 September 2013|publisher=[[Pandora Archive]] ([[National Library of Australia]])|accessdate=7 May 2014}}</ref>

The single was officially released on 6 November 2015.

==Video==
Despite "Ba*Bing" not being released as an official single, a video clip premiered on Brown's [[VEVO]] account on Monday 8 September 2014, almost a year after it was first digitally released. The video was included on the single "[[Better Not Said]]", which was released in Australia on 12 September 2014.<ref>https://itunes.apple.com/au/album/better-not-said-ep/id910150714</ref>
