'''Pamięcin''' {{IPAc-pl|p|a|'|m|j|e|n|ć|i|n}} ({{lang-de|Frauendorf}}) is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Górzyca]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland, close to the [[Germany|German]] border.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> 

It lies approximately {{convert|5|km|mi|0}} south of [[Górzyca, Lubusz Voivodeship|Górzyca]], {{convert|13|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of [[Słubice]], {{convert|52|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and {{convert|82|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of Zielona Góra.

The village has a population of 410.
