'''Bronowice''' {{IPAc-pl|b|r|o|n|o|'|w|i|c|e}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Strzelce Krajeńskie]], within [[Strzelce-Drezdenko County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|4|km|mi|0}} north-west of [[Strzelce Krajeńskie]] and {{convert|25|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-east of [[Gorzów Wielkopolski]].

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').
