'''Cyriacus I''' (? – 230) succeeded [[Philadelphus of Byzantium|Philadelphus]] as [[Bishop]] of [[Byzantium]] and governed the local [[local church|church]] for 16 years. In some catalogues appears under the name '''Cyrillianus'''.
