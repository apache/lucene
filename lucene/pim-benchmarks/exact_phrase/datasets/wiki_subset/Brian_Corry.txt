'''Brian Corry''' (born 1988 in [[Riverstick]], [[County Cork]]) is an [[Republic of Ireland|Irish]] sportsperson.  He plays [[hurling]] with his local club [[Ballymartle GAA|Ballymartle]] and with the [[Cork GAA|Cork]] senior inter-county team.<ref>{{cite news|title=Tipp put an end to 86-year hoodoo|url=http://www.independent.ie/sport/hurling/tipp-put-end-to-86year-hoodoo-1402391.html|accessdate=3 January 2011|newspaper=[[Irish Independent]]|date=9 June 2008}}</ref>

==Playing career==
===Club===

Corry plays [[hurling]] with his local club in [[Ballymartle GAA|Ballymartle]]. He has enjoyed some success with his club winning the [[Cork Intermediate Hurling Championship]] in 2006.

===Inter-county===

Corry first came to prominence on the inter-county scene as a member of the [[Cork GAA|Cork]] minor hurling team in 2006.  That year All-Ireland final.
