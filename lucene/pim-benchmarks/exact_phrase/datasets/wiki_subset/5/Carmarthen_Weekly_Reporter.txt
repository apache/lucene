'''''The Carmarthen Weekly Reporter''''' was a weekly liberal, English language newspaper, published in [[Carmarthen]] and distributed throughout [[South Wales]]. It contained local, national and foreign news, and local information. It was published by William Morgan Evans.<ref name=":0">[http://newspapers.library.wales/browse/3581813 Carmarthen Weekly Reporter] at Welsh Newspapers Online, National Library of Wales</ref>

It ceased publication in 1921, partly as a result of competition from the ''[[Carmarthen Journal]]'' and ''[[The Welshman (newspaper)|The Welshman]]'', which were also published in Carmarthen.

[[Welsh Newspapers Online]] has digitised 1,840 issues of the Carmarthen Weekly Reporter (1860-1919) from the newspaper holdings of the [[National Library of Wales]].<ref name=":0" />
