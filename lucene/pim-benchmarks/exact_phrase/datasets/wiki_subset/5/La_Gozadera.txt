"'''La Gozadera'''" (Spanish term meaning a "good time" or "party") is a song by Cuban duo [[Gente de Zona]] as the lead single for their third studio album [[Visualízate]]. Featuring American singer [[Marc Anthony]], it was released on April 30, 2015 by [[Sony Music Latin]]. The music video for the song was  nominated in the Videoclip Awards 2016, The music video was directed by Alejandro Pérez and filmed in Cuba while the scenes with Marc Anthony were filmed in the Dominican Republic.<ref>{{cite news|last1=Saldana|first1=Janel|title=Gente De Zona 'La Gozadera' Feat. Marc Anthony: When, Where To Watch New Music Video!|url=http://www.latintimes.com/gente-de-zona-la-gozadera-feat-marc-anthony-when-where-watch-new-music-video-320513|accessdate=August 18, 2015|work=Latin Times|publisher=[[IBT Media]]|date=June 3, 2015}}</ref> The song was produced by Motiff, a [[salsa music|salsa]] version was also recorded which was produced by [[Sergio George]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Gente de Zona estrena en versión salsa de "La Gozadera"|url=http://www.htv.com/noticias/gente-de-zona-estrena-en-version-salsa-de-la-gozadera|website=[[HispanicTV]]|accessdate=August 18, 2015|language=Spanish|date=August 3, 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150808215030/http://www.htv.com/noticias/gente-de-zona-estrena-en-version-salsa-de-la-gozadera|archive-date=August 8, 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> As of November 2019, the video has received over 1.2 billion views on [[YouTube]].<ref>GenteDeZonaVevo. [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMp55KH_3wo "Gente de Zona - La Gozadera"], YouTube, June 5, 2015, accessed July 7, 2017</ref>

==In other media==
"La Gozadera" was featured in 2017 Chilean animated film ''Condorito: La Película''.
