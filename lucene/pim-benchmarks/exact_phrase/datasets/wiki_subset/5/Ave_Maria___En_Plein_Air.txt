'''''Ave Maria''''' is the first classical, and the fifth studio album released by the [[Finland|Finnish]] [[soprano]] [[Tarja Turunen]].<ref name="AveMariaEnPleinAir">{{cite web|url=http://www.tarja-avemaria.com/ |title=New Album "Ave Maria - En Plein Air"|publisher=Tarja-avemaria.com|accessdate=2015-08-24}}</ref>

On the 22 July 2015, earMUSIC released a video announcing the existence of the album, following with a teaser trailer on 6 August. The full music video was not released until 13 August that year, with Tarja singing the rare "Ave Maria" by [[Paolo Tosti]].

==Background==
In an interview with Winter Storm Slovakia, Tarja talked a little about the recording sessions :

"All the musicians performed the songs together with me without an audience" and continues saying "Nothing has been edited nor any studio tricks have been used" and finishes with "So what you hear on the record, is purely how the songs sounded in Lakeuden Risti church during the recording session." <ref name="winterstormslovakia.com">{{cite web|url=http://www.winterstormslovakia.com/english.html |title=English - Winter Storm Slovakia &#124; Oficiálny fanklub Tarji Turunen na Slovensku |publisher=Winter Storm Slovakia |date= |accessdate=2015-08-24}}</ref>

She also says that this is a very important album to her, since "It represents her long background in classical music and her knowledge as a [[Lyric soprano|lyrical singer]]".
