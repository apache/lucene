'''Ragnhild Jacobsson''' (born 15 July 1966) is a [[Norwegians|Norwegian]] entrepreneur, social worker, and [[Environmental Activist|environmental activist]]. <ref>{{cite news|title=Slåss för djur: "Fyller själen med glädje"|publisher=AB Kvällstidningen Expressen|url=http://www.expressen.se/gt/slass-for-djur-fyller-sjalen-med-gladje|accessdate=19 August 2015}}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Jacobsson is married to [[Lars Valentin Jacobsson]] and the couple supports animal projects globally.<ref>{{cite news|title=Bilder: Jippot som ska rädda noshörningar – Göteborgs-Posten|url=http://m.gp.se/nyheter/goteborg/1.2492992-bilder-jippot-som-ska-radda-noshorningar|accessdate=19 August 2015}}</ref>

== Philanthropy ==
Ragnhild and her husband Lars have been working on animal projects since 2000. They bought Volunteer Travels Ltd. in 2008 and founded [[The Perfect World Foundation]] in 2010. <ref>{{cite news|title=Historia – The Perfect World Foundation|url=https://theperfectworldfoundation.se/vilka-vi-ar/organisation/historia/|accessdate=19 August 2015}}</ref>The organizations are now working on more than 80 wildlife and environmental projects in 23 countries. In 2014, Jacobsson, under the umbrella of The Perfect World Foundation, arranged a wildlife gala and conference in [[Scandinavia]]. The event was titled ''Save the Rhino 2014'' and was, at the time, the largest of its type in the region.{{Citation needed|date=September 2020}} [[Mark Shand]] who was among the advisors to hold this event was the guest of honour and was awarded the "Conservationist of Year 2014" award and the "Fragile Rhino".<ref>{{cite news|title=Mr Mark Shand Awarded with|publisher=theperfectworldfoundation.org|url=https://theperfectworldfoundation.org/mark-shand-awarded-conservationist-year-2014-perfect-world-foundation/|accessdate=10 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Göteborgsparet ska åka Tuk Tuk i Indien med prins Charles|url=http://flytid.me/2015/04/09/goteborgsparet-ska-aka-tuk-tuk-i-indien-med-prins-charles/|url-status=dead|accessdate=24 August 2015|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20150824192534/http://flytid.me/2015/04/09/goteborgsparet-ska-aka-tuk-tuk-i-indien-med-prins-charles/|archivedate=24 August 2015|df=dmy-all}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Prins Charles deltar i indiskt tuk-tuk-lopp till förmån för den asiatiska elefanten|url=http://lyx.se/prins-charles-tuk-tuk-elefant/|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Prins Charles tar emot Lars och Ragnhild Jacobsson i prinsparets privata hem i London.|url=http://www.hittarekryterare.se/news/pressrelease/id/827128/prins-charles-tar-emot-lars-och-ragnhild-jacobsson-i-prinsparets-privata-hem-i-london|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=The Prince of Wales And Duchess of Cornwall Launch 'Travels to My Elephant' Rickshaw Race|publisher=Getty Images|url=http://www.gettyimages.se/detail/nyhetsfoto/prince-charles-prince-of-wales-and-camilla-duchess-of-nyhetsfoto/467629796|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref>

Both Ragnhild and Lars are ambassadors of the London-based organisation "[[Elephant Family]]" and are actively involved in the conservation of [[Asian elephant|Asian elephants]]. <ref>{{cite news|title=Svenskparets resa med prins Charles|publisher=AB Kvällstidningen Expressen|url=http://www.expressen.se/gt/svenskparets-resa-med-prins-charles/|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Dalaelefant räddar utrotningshotade djur|url=http://www.dt.se/dalarna/mora/dalaelefant-raddar-utrotningshotade-djur|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Our Team – Elephant Family|url=http://elephant-family.org/who-we-are/our-team|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref>
