'''Lars Jacobsson''', (born 11 May 1965), is a Swedish [[entrepreneurship|entrepreneur]],<ref>{{cite news|title=Svensker blev millionær på at spekulere i olie - nu vil han redde verden med solenergi - Politiken.dk|url=http://politiken.dk/magasinet/feature/ECE2544434/svensker-blev-millionaer-paa-at-spekulere-i-olie---nu-vil-han-redde-verden-med-solenergi/|accessdate=19 August 2015|publisher=Vox}}</ref> [[Invention|inventor]],<ref>{{cite news|title=Why speculators are stashing vast quantities of crude oil on tanker ships|url=https://www.vox.com/2014/12/25/7443569/contango-oil-storage|accessdate=19 August 2015|publisher=Vox}}</ref> [[Philanthropy|philanthropist]], and [[Conservation movement|conservationist]].

Jacobsson originally worked in the [[Petroleum industry|oil business]],<ref>{{citation |title= Lars Jacobsson: Executive Profile & Biography - Businessweek|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/people/person.asp?personId=260438611|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Bloomberg}}</ref> where he was involved in starting up storage, trading and shipping companies, as well as being the [[Invention|inventor]] of the oil [[Contango]] market in the early 1990s.<ref>{{cite news|title=Understanding the concept Contango, backwardation, convenience yield in Financial Derivatives|url=http://www.slideshare.net/geetasaravate45/contango-backwardation-convenience-yield|accessdate=19 August 2015|publisher=Vox}}</ref><ref>{{citation |title=Gold/Mining/Energy |url=http://www.siliconinvestor.com/readmsg.aspx?msgid=29968394|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Silicon Investor}}</ref><ref>{{citation |title=Oil barrel price|url=http://evgenykoma.com/oil-barrel-price/|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref>

In early 2000s he sold his oil related businesses.<ref>{{cite news|title=Bilder: Jippot som ska rädda noshörningar - Göteborgs-Posten|url=http://m.gp.se/nyheter/goteborg/1.2492992-bilder-jippot-som-ska-radda-noshorningar|accessdate=19 August 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Historia - The Perfect World Foundation|url=https://theperfectworldfoundation.se/vilka-vi-ar/organisation/historia/|accessdate=19 August 2015}}</ref> Since 2005, he has, among others, been involved in starting up two solar energy companies.<ref>{{cite news|title=Duon som kan fixa tusentals nya jobb|url=http://www.expressen.se/gt/duon-som-kan-fixa-tusentals-nya-jobb/|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=AB Kvällstidningen Expressen}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Ubåtsteknik kan ge svenska bolag ny miljardindustri|url=http://www.svd.se/ubatsteknik-kan-ge-svenska-bolag-ny-miljardindustri|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Svenska Dagbladet}}</ref> Jacobsson's company [[United Sun Systems International]] develops solar energy technology.

==Charity==
Together with his wife [[Ragnhild Jacobsson|Ragnhild (Rags) Jacobsson]], he founded [[The Perfect World Foundation]], a non-profit and independent Swedish based organisation that promotes conservation. In 2014 the organisation held the largest ever, in Scandinavia, fundraising gala and wildlife & environmental conference<ref>{{cite news|title=Gothenburg hosts prestigious charity gala|url=http://www.goteborgdaily.se/gothenburg-hosts-prestigious-charity-gala|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=Göteborg Daily|url-status=dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20160927041546/http://www.goteborgdaily.se/gothenburg-hosts-prestigious-charity-gala|archivedate=27 September 2016}}</ref> in favour of the [[Endangered species|endangered]] [[Rhinoceros|rhinos]] and [[elephant]]s, with participants from all over the world. Jacobbson and his wife working with, as ambassadors, the London-based organisation [[Elephant Family]] to save the [[asian elephant]]s.<ref>{{cite news|title=Mr Mark Shand Awarded with|url=https://theperfectworldfoundation.org/mark-shand-awarded-conservationist-year-2014-perfect-world-foundation/|accessdate=10 August 2015|publisher=theperfectworldfoundation.org}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Göteborgsparet ska åka Tuk Tuk i Indien med prins Charles|url=http://flytid.me/2015/04/09/goteborgsparet-ska-aka-tuk-tuk-i-indien-med-prins-charles/|accessdate=24 August 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150824192534/http://flytid.me/2015/04/09/goteborgsparet-ska-aka-tuk-tuk-i-indien-med-prins-charles/|archive-date=24 August 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Prins Charles deltar i indiskt tuk-tuk-lopp till förmån för den asiatiska elefanten|url=http://lyx.se/prins-charles-tuk-tuk-elefant/|accessdate=24 August 2015}}</ref>

On Sunday, June 7, 2015 at a private event in Stockholm, Mr and Mrs Jacobsson on behalf of The Perfect World Foundation honored [[Jane Goodall]] with "The Fragile Rhino" prize along with naming her conservationist of the year for 2015.<ref>{{cite news|title=Jane Honored as Conservationist of the Year|url=http://www.janegoodall.org/blogs/jane-honored-conservationist-year|accessdate=24 August 2015|publisher=the Jane Goodall Institute}}</ref>

In September 2016 Mr & Mrs Jacobsson organised a large fundraising event "Save The Elephant", a ball with participants from all over the world. During the event [[Richard Leakey]], behind the largest ivory burning ever in Kenya,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.svd.se/over-hundra-ton-elfenben-ska-brannas|title=Jättebrasa av elfenben – väntas brinna i dagar|date=April 30, 2016|via=www.svd.se}}</ref> was awarded "The Conservationist of the year 2016" by the organisation's new ambassador, the [[Duchess of York]].{{cn|date=March 2020}}
