'''Agassiz''' is an [[impact crater]] on [[Mars]], named in honor of geologist [[Louis Agassiz]] (1807–1873).<ref>{{cite book | last1=Schmadel | first=Lutz D. | title=Dictionary of Minor Planet Names | publisher=Springer Science & Business Media | year=2012 | isbn=978-3642297182 | page=176 | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VoJ5nUyIzCsC }}</ref> The name was approved in 1973 by the [[International Astronomical Union]] (IAU) Working Group for Planetary System Nomenclature (WGPSN).<ref name=GPN /> The crater is {{convert|108.77|km}} in diameter<ref name="crater"/> and is located at 69.9°S 88.9°W.
