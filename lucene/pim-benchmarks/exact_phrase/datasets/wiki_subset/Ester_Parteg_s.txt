'''Ester Partegàs''' (born 1972 in [[La Garriga]], [[Province of Barcelona|Barcelona]], Spain)<ref name="Boucher">{{cite journal |last1=Boucher |first1=Brian |title=Review: Ester Partegas  New York at Foxy Production |journal=Art in America |date=28 January 2011 |url=https://www.artinamericamagazine.com/reviews/ester-partegas/ |accessdate=17 April 2019}}</ref> is a Spanish contemporary artist who lives and works in [[Brooklyn]], [[New York (state)|New York]]. She has an M.F.A. (sculpture) from the [[Universitat de Barcelona]] and has completed postgraduate studies at [[Hochschule der Künste]] in Berlin. She participated in the International Studio and Curatorial Program, New York, in 1999.

Her work has been exhibited internationally, including the [[Aldrich Contemporary Art Museum]] (Connecticut), [[Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofía]] (Madrid), 2nd Moscow Bienniale, [[Weatherspoon Art Museum]] (Greensboro, North Carolina), Walker's Point Center for the Arts (Milwaukee, Wisconsin), [[Virginia Commonwealth University]] (Richmond, Virginia), Cercle Cultural Caja Madrid (Barcelona), [[SculptureCenter]] (New York), [[Rice University]] Art Gallery (Houston, Texas), [[Queens Museum of Art]] (New York) and [[Whitney Museum of American Art]] (Altria, New York). Her work is also in numerous private collections in the US and Europe.
