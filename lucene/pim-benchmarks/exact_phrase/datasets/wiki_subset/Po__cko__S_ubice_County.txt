'''Połęcko''' {{IPAc-pl|p|o|'|ł|e|n|c|k|o}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Ośno Lubuskie]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) &ndash; TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|5|km|mi|0}} south of [[Ośno Lubuskie]], {{convert|24|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} east of [[Słubice]], {{convert|43|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of [[Gorzów Wielkopolski]], and {{convert|68|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of [[Zielona Góra]].

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').

The village has a population of 300.
