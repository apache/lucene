'''''Ornithophila''''' are a [[genus]] of biting [[fly|flies]] in the family of louse flies, [[Hippoboscidae]]. There are two known species.<ref name="Dick06">{{Cite journal| author = Dick, C. W.| year = 2006| title = Checklist of World Hippoboscidae (Diptera: Hippoboscoidea).| journal =  | place = Chicago| publisher = Department of Zoology, Field Museum of Natural History| volume = | issue = | pages = 1–7pp| issn =}}</ref> Both species are [[parasitism|parasites]] of [[bird]]s.<ref name="Maa69">{{Cite journal| author = Maa, T. C.| year = 1969| title = A Revised Checklist and Concise Host Index of Hippoboscidae (Diptera)| journal = Pacific Insects Monograph | place = Honolulu| publisher = Bishop Museum, Honolulu, Hawaii| volume = 20| issue =| pages = 261–299pp| issn =}}</ref>

==Distribution==
''Ornithophila'' are found worldwide with the exception of the [[Americas]] and [[Antarctica]].<ref name="Maa69"/>
