'''Rzepinek''' {{IPAc-pl|ż|e|'|p|i|n|e|k}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Rzepin]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> 

It lies approximately {{convert|3|km|mi|0}} south of [[Rzepin]], {{convert|19|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} east of [[Słubice]], {{convert|54|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and {{convert|63|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of Zielona Góra.

The village has a population of 10.
