'''Małe Osiedle''' {{IPAc-pl|'|m|a|ł|e|-|o|'|ś|e|d|l|e}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Strzelce Krajeńskie]], within [[Strzelce-Drezdenko County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref>

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').
