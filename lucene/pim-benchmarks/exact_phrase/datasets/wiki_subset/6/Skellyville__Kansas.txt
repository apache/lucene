'''Skellyville''' is an [[unincorporated area|unincorporated community]] in [[Kingman County, Kansas|Kingman County]], [[Kansas]], United States.<ref name="GNIS"/>  It is located approximately {{convert|2|mi|km}} northwest of [[Cunningham, Kansas|Cunningham]] on the north side of the [[South Fork Ninnescah River]].

==History==
Skellyville was formed as an "oil town" when oil was discovered in the area.  As the pocket of oil dried up, the population decreased as well.
