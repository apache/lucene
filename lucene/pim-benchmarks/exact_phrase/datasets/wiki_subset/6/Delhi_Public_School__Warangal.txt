'''Delhi Public School, Warangal''' (commonly known as DPS Warangal) is a day and residential [[Central Board of Secondary Education|CBSE]] school with more than 1200 students. The school started functioning on 19 June 2014. It is operated by [[Delhi Public School Society]], [[New Delhi]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Complete Information Form |url=http://cbseaff.nic.in/cbse_aff/schdir_Report/AppViewdir.aspx?affno=130376 |access-date=2020-08-19 |website=CBSE}}</ref>
