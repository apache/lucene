The '''1990–91 NSL Cup''' was the fifteenth season of the [[NSL Cup]], which was the main national [[association football]] [[single-elimination tournament|knockout]] cup competition in [[Australia]]. 14 teams from around Australia entered the competition.<ref>http://www.worldfootball.net/all_matches/aus-australia-cup-1990-1991</ref>
