'''Heinrich Unkel, [[Order of Friars Minor|O.F.M.]]''' or '''Arnold von Unkel''' (died 22 Jan 1482) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as [[Bishop of Cologne|Auxiliary Bishop of Cologne]] (1481–1482).<ref name=HierarchiaII-Cyrene />

==Biography==
Heinrich Unkel was appointed a priest in the [[Order of Friars Minor]].<ref name=CathHierHeinUnkel /> In 1481, he was appointed during the papacy of [[Pope Sixtus IV]] as [[Bishop of Cologne|Auxiliary Bishop of Cologne]] and [[Titular Bishop]] of ''Cyrene''.<ref name=HierarchiaII-Cyrene /><ref name=CathHierHeinUnkel /> He died on 22 Jan 1482 before he was installed.<ref name=CathHierHeinUnkel />
