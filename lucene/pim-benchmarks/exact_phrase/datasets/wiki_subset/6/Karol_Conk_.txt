'''Karol Conka''' (born '''Karoline dos Santos Oliveira'''; January 1, 1987) is a  [[Brazilian hip hop|Brazilian rapper]], songwriter, actress, record producer, model and television presenter. Born in [[Curitiba]], Conka is one of the most prominent female rappers in Brazil today.<ref>{{citation |url=http://www.smh.com.au/entertainment/music/brazilian-hiphop-artist-karol-conka-set-to-rock-the-domain-for-free-concert-20150109-12jwpi.html |title=Brazilian hip-hop artist Karol Conka set to rock the Domain for free concert |work=[[The Sydney Morning Herald]] |date=January 9, 2015 }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2014/jun/19/sao-paulo-every-day-monday-every-night-friday |title=São Paulo's music scene: 'Every day is Monday, every night is Friday' |work=[[The Guardian]] |date=June 19, 2014 }}</ref> Her music is a blend of modern hip-hop and rap with sounds from Brazilian pop and traditional music.<ref>{{citation |url=http://www1.folha.uol.com.br/ilustrada/2013/07/1319023-critica-com-batuk-freak-karol-conka-avanca-e-deixa-de-ser-aposta.shtml |title=Crítica: Com 'Batuk Freak', Karol Conka avança e deixa de see aposta |date=July 31, 2013 |work=[[Folha de S.Paulo]] }}</ref><ref>{{citation |url=http://www.refinery29.com/2015/07/91465/karol-conka-brazilian-rapper |date=July 28, 2015 |work=[[Refinery29]] |title=Brazilian Artist Karol Conka Opens Up About Discrimination, Self-Esteem & Personal Style }}</ref> In 2013, she won [[Multishow]]'s Best New Artist award and released her debut album, ''Batuk Freak''.<ref>{{citation |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/pictures/10-new-artists-you-need-to-know-april-2014-20140410/karol-conka-0274380 |title=10 New Artists You Need to Know: April 2014 |work=[[Rolling Stone]] }}</ref> Her song, "Boa Noite", is featured in the [[EA Sports]] video game, ''[[FIFA 14]]''.

== Personal life ==
