'''''Matelea obliqua''''', commonly known as '''climbing milkvine''',<ref>{{PLANTS|id=MAOB2|taxon=Matelea obliqua|accessdate=19 June 2016}}</ref> '''limerock milkvine''' or '''northern spinypod''', is a species of flowering plant in the [[Apocynaceae|dogbane family]]. It a twining herbaceous vine that produces maroon flowers in summer.

It is native to the eastern United States,<ref>{{BONAP|ref|genus=Matelea|species=obliqua|accessdate=19 June 2016}}</ref> where it is found in areas of [[calcareous]] rocky woodland.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.herbarium.unc.edu/flora.htm |title= Flora of the Southern and Mid-Atlantic States}}</ref> It is generally uncommon throughout its range, and is found in low densities.
