'''Rosławice''' {{IPAc-pl|r|o|s|ł|a|'|w|i|c|e}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Ośno Lubuskie]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref>

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').

The village has a population of 10.
