'''''Tagetes subulata''''' is an herbaceous plant of the sunflower family. It is widespread across most of [[Mexico]], and found also in [[Central America]], [[Colombia]], and [[Venezuela]].<ref>[http://www.globalspecies.org/ntaxa/2272918 Global Species - Tagetes subulata]</ref> It has highly divided bright green leaves and yellow flowers contained in an elongated calyx.<ref>[http://www.madrean.org/maba/symbflora/taxa/index.php?taxon=28469 Madrean Archipelago Biodiversity Assessment Flora - Tagetes subulata]</ref><ref>Turner, B. L. 1996. The Comps of Mexico: A systematic account of the family Asteraceae, vol. 6. Tageteae and Athemideae. Phytologia Memoirs. 10: i–ii, 1–22, 43–93</ref><ref>Williams, L. O. 1976. Tribe VI, Helenieae. En: D. L. Nash & L. O. Williams (Eds), Flora of Guatemala - Part XII. Fieldiana, Botany 24(12): 361–386, 571–580</ref><ref>Hokche, O., P. E. Berry & O. Huber. (eds.) 2008. Nuevo Catálogo de la Flora Vascular de Venezuela 1–859. Fundación Instituto Botánico de Venezuela, Caracas</ref><ref>Strother, J. L. 1999. Compositae–Heliantheae s. l. 5: 1–232. In D.E.
