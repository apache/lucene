'''Keshvar Rural District''' ({{lang-fa|دهستان كشور}}) is a [[Rural Districts of Iran|rural district]] (''dehestan'') in [[Papi District]], [[Khorramabad County]], [[Lorestan Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,873, in 418 families.<ref>{{IranCensus2006|15}}</ref>  The rural district has 43 villages.
