'''Rot in the Sun''' is a song by [[United States|American]] [[alternative rock]] band [[The Presidents of the United States of America (band)|The Presidents of the United States of America]]. It was released as a single on December 23, 2008 on Fugitive Records. It is the third single from the album ''[[These Are the Good Times People]]''.

==Music video==
The music video for "Rot in the Sun" features band members [[Chris Ballew]], [[Andrew McKeag]] and [[Jason Finn (musician)|Jason Finn]] as paper dolls playing their instruments in front of a coloured paper background. Their faces were filmed and then [[superimposed]] onto the recording of the paper dolls.
