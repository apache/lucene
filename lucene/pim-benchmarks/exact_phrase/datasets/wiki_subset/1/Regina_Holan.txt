'''Regina Holan''' is an [[United States|American]] [[women's soccer|football]] [[Striker (association football)|striker]] of [[Czechoslovakia|Czech]] descent. She played for [[Portland Pilots]] and [[California Golden Bears]] in the [[NCAA]],<ref>[http://www.calbears.com/sports/w-soccer/mtt/holan_regina00.html Profile] in [[California Golden Bears]]' website</ref> [[AC Sparta Prague (women)|Sparta Prague]] in the [[Czech First Division (women)|Czech First Division]], and [[San Francisco Nighthawks]] in the [[Women's Premier Soccer League|WPSL]].<ref>[http://www.sfnighthawks.com/schedulea.php SFN Scorers between 1999–2006] in the club's website</ref> With Sparta she took part in the [[UEFA Women's Cup]].<ref>[http://www.uefa.com/womenschampionsleague/news/newsid=35848.html Umeå on form.] [[UEFA]]</ref>

Married with a [[Greece|Greek]] citizen, she was a member of the [[Greece women's national football team|Greek national team]].<ref>[http://articles.sfgate.com/2004-05-30/sports/17427417_1_greek-coach-czech-republic-greece She plays for Greece, by way of Los Altos.] [[San Francisco Chronicle]]</ref>
