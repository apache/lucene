'''Drzeńsko''' {{IPAc-pl|'|d|ż|e|ń|s|k|o}} (formerly German ''Drenzig'') is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Rzepin]], within [[Słubice County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland.<ref name="TERYT">{{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}}</ref> It lies approximately {{convert|4|km|mi|0}} north-west of [[Rzepin]], {{convert|15|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} east of [[Słubice]], {{convert|52|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of Gorzów Wielkopolski, and {{convert|69|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north-west of Zielona Góra.

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').

The village has a population of 450.
