Stadio Tullio Saleri (formerly '''Nuovo Stadio Comunale''') is a multi-use [[stadium]] in [[Lumezzane]], [[Italy]].  It is currently used mostly for [[football (soccer)|football]] matches and is the home ground of [[A.C. Lumezzane]].  The stadium holds 4,150.
