'''Yad Rambam''' ({{lang-he-n|יַד רַמְבַּ"ם}}, ''lit.'' Rambam Memorial) is a [[moshav]] in central [[Israel]]. Located in the [[Shephelah]] near [[Ramle]], it falls under the jurisdiction of [[Gezer Regional Council]]. In {{Israel populations|Year}} it had a population of {{Israel populations|Yad Rambam}}.{{Israel populations|reference}}

==History==
The moshav was founded on 6 November 1955 and was named after [[Maimonides]] (known in Hebrew as Rambam). Most of the founders were [[Jewish exodus from Arab lands|immigrants]] from [[Fes, Morocco|Fes]] in [[Morocco]].
