'''Dalian Polytechnic University''' ({{zh|s=大连工业大学|p=Dàlián Gōngyè Dàxué}}) is a university in [[Dalian]], [[Liaoning]].

==History==
The school was founded in 1958 in [[Shenyang]] as the Shenyang Institute of Light Industry ({{zh|s=沈阳工业大学|p=Shěnyáng Gōngyè Dàxué}}). In 1970, it was transferred to Dalian, and renamed as the Dalian Institute of Light Industry ({{zh|s=大连轻工业学院|p=Dàlián Qīnggōngyè Xuéyuàn}}). In 2006, it was promoted in 2006 from the rank of [[institute]] to the rank of [[university]].

It is the only institution of higher education in northeast of China specializing in [[light industry]], [[food]], [[textile]]s and [[art design]]. There are about 15,000 students on the {{convert|85|acre|m2|adj=on}} campus. Subjects taught include [[engineering]], [[science]], [[art]]s, [[management]], and [[economics]].
